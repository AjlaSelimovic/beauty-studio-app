About Us

Beauty First is more than a beauty salon, it is a place where natural beauty and magic come together, an oasis of peace and relaxation.
Beauty salon "Beauty First" was opened in March 2019 in the heart of Sarajevo. Completely renovated and equipped space, it has slowly taken the form of an oasis for relaxation and rejuvenation, where each client is approached individually.
We offer facial treatments, massages, hairdressing services, manicures and pedicures, waxing and more.
Our database of satisfied clients crosses the borders of Bosnia and Herzegovina.
We invite you to visit our salon and go through a unique experience .
